 Individual donations and community crowdfunding alone will not sustain operations. ERA figured this out and went B2B. Siren's equivalent revenue model is not yet fully defined. The Paystack donation split is a nice community mechanic but not a business. The business is likely: corporate location subscriptions (companies subscribing Guardian Mode for all employees' home areas), HMO partnerships (health insurers paying for coordination when their members are near an incident), and eventually a verified responder certification programme that companies pay to have their staff hold. These are the revenue lines worth thinking about now even if you don't build them until Year 2.
